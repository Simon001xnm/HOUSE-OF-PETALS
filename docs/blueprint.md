# **App Name**: House of Petals

## Core Features:

- Product Catalog: Browse a curated collection of flowers and gifts with high-quality images and clear categories.
- Product Detail Pages: View detailed information for each product, including description, price, available options, and customer reviews.
- Shopping Cart: Manage selected items, adjust quantities, and remove products before checkout.
- Secure Checkout: Complete purchases through a secure and streamlined process, including shipping and payment options.
- AI Gift Advisor: A generative AI tool that recommends personalized gift suggestions based on occasion, recipient, and user-input preferences.
- Order Tracking: View current and past order history, along with real-time status updates.
- User Authentication: Allow users to create accounts, log in, and manage their profile details for a personalized shopping experience.

## Style Guidelines:

- The primary color is a deep, rich forest green (#296133), evoking nature and luxury, similar to the main 'H' and text in the logo.
- The background color is a very dark, subtle grey-green (#1B1F1C), providing a sophisticated canvas that makes the products stand out.
- The accent color is a vibrant, deep red (#AD1E37), reflecting the passionate hue of the rose in the logo and used for key highlights and call-to-actions. The gold elements from the logo should be incorporated subtly for borders and small decorative touches.
- Headline font: 'Alegreya' (serif) for an elegant, literary and luxurious feel. Body text font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for clean readability and a modern aesthetic.
- Use sophisticated, minimal line-art icons that complement the elegant branding, possibly with subtle floral motifs or gold accents for premium features.
- A clean, spacious layout with ample whitespace, focusing on showcasing high-quality product photography. Employ a flexible grid system for product displays and a refined, intuitive navigation structure.
- Subtle and smooth transitions for page loads and component interactions, such as graceful fades for image galleries and gentle hover effects for product cards, enhancing the luxurious feel without distracting.
